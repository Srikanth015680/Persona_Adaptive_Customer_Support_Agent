
# Billing Overview

This article explains how billing works in CloudSuite, including payment schedules, invoices, payment failures, and updating payment information.

## Billing Schedule

CloudSuite offers both monthly and annual subscriptions.

* Monthly plans are charged once per month on the same date you originally subscribed.
* Annual plans are charged once per year on your renewal date.

You can view your current billing cycle at any time from the Billing section of your account.

## Viewing Invoices

An invoice is generated after every successful payment.

To view previous invoices:

1. Open  **Billing** .
2. Select  **Invoice History** .
3. Download any invoice as a PDF.

Invoices remain available in your account and can be downloaded whenever needed.

## Supported Payment Methods

CloudSuite currently supports:

* Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
* PayPal (Starter and Pro plans)
* ACH bank transfers (Enterprise plans)

Available payment options may vary depending on your subscription plan.

## What Happens if a Payment Fails?

A payment can fail for several reasons, such as an expired card, insufficient funds, or bank restrictions.

When this happens:

* CloudSuite automatically retries the payment.
* You'll receive an email notification about the failed charge.
* You can update your payment method and retry the payment at any time.

If payment cannot be collected after multiple attempts, the account may be temporarily moved to read-only mode until the outstanding balance is paid.

## Duplicate Charges

If you believe you were charged twice for the same billing period, contact Billing Support before opening a dispute with your bank.

When contacting support, include:

* The transaction IDs (if available)
* The date of the charges
* A screenshot of the billing statement

The billing team will review the transactions and process any necessary refund if a duplicate charge is confirmed.

## Updating Payment Information

To update your payment method:

1. Open  **Billing** .
2. Select  **Payment Methods** .
3. Add a new payment method or edit an existing one.
4. Save your changes.

Updates take effect immediately for future billing cycles.
