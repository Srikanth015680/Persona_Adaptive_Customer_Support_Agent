
# Subscription Plans and Pricing

CloudSuite offers three subscription plans designed for individuals, teams, and large organizations.

## Available Plans

| Plan       | Monthly Billing                         | Annual Billing     | Seats           |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------- | ------------------ | --------------- |
| Starter    | $19/month          | $190/year          | 1 user             |                 |
| Pro        | $49/month per user | $470/year per user | Flexible team size |                 |
| Enterprise | Custom pricing                          | Custom pricing     | Unlimited users |

## Plan Features

### Starter

Includes:

* Core ticket management
* 1 GB storage
* Email support
* Up to 60 API requests per minute

### Pro

Includes everything in Starter, plus:

* Team collaboration features
* 50 GB storage
* Live chat support
* Up to 300 API requests per minute
* Webhook integrations

### Enterprise

Includes everything in Pro, plus:

* Single Sign-On (SSO)
* SAML support
* Dedicated account manager
* Custom service agreements
* Up to 2,000 API requests per minute
* Unlimited storage
* Priority phone support

## Upgrading Your Plan

You can upgrade your subscription at any time.

When you upgrade:

* New features become available immediately.
* Any pricing adjustment is automatically calculated during checkout.
* No manual action is required.

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Before downgrading, make sure your account stays within the limits of the lower plan. For example:

* Storage usage must be within the allowed limit.
* Team size must meet the plan requirements.

If limits are exceeded, the downgrade may not be applied until the issue is resolved.

## Switching Billing Frequency

You can change between monthly and annual billing from:

**Billing → Plan Details**

Changes made during an active billing period are automatically adjusted by the billing system.

## Need Help Choosing a Plan?

If you're unsure which plan is best for your team, contact Sales or Support for guidance.
