
# Account Lockout and Recovery

If you're unable to access your CloudSuite account because it has been locked, this guide explains why it happened and how to regain access.

## Why Was My Account Locked?

CloudSuite locks accounts to help protect customer data and prevent unauthorized access.

Common reasons include:

* Multiple incorrect password attempts in a short period of time
* Suspicious login activity detected by our security systems
* Sign-in attempts from an unfamiliar location or device

In most cases, account lockouts are temporary and can be resolved without contacting support.

## Temporary Lockouts

If your account was locked after several failed login attempts, it will automatically unlock after 30 minutes.

During this time:

* Do not continue attempting to sign in
* Make sure you are using the correct password
* Use the Password Reset option if you're unsure of your credentials

Once the lockout period ends, you can sign in normally.

## Unlock Your Account

You may be able to unlock your account immediately by following these steps:

1. Go to the login page.
2. Select  **Unlock My Account** .
3. Complete the identity verification process.
4. Enter the verification code sent to your recovery email or phone number.
5. Sign in again using your existing password.

You do not need to reset your password unless you have forgotten it.

## Manual Security Review

Some lockouts require additional verification.

If you receive a message stating  **"Manual Review Required"** , our security systems have flagged the account for further investigation.

These cases cannot be resolved through self-service tools.

Typical reasons include:

* Login attempts from unusual locations
* Suspicious device activity
* Potential account compromise

The Trust & Safety team will review the account and typically responds within one business day.

## Tips to Avoid Future Lockouts

To reduce the chances of being locked out again:

* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
* Keep your recovery email and phone number up to date
* Use a trusted device whenever possible
* Avoid repeatedly entering incorrect passwords
* Reset your password if you're unsure of your login details
