
# Password Reset

If you've forgotten your CloudSuite password, you can reset it from the login page in a few minutes.

## Reset Your Password

1. Open the CloudSuite login page.
2. Click  **Forgot Password** .
3. Enter the email address linked to your account.
4. Check your inbox for a password reset email.
5. Open the reset link and create a new password.
6. Sign in using your new password.

**Note:** Password reset links remain active for 30 minutes. Only the latest reset link will work.

## Didn't Receive the Reset Email?

Try the following:

* Check your Spam or Junk folder.
* Make sure you entered the correct email address.
* Wait a few minutes and refresh your inbox.
* Add `cloudsuite-mail.com` to your safe-sender list.
* Request a new reset email if the previous one has not arrived after 15 minutes.

Reset emails are sent from:

`no-reply@cloudsuite-mail.com`

## Reset Link Expired

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 30 minutes.

If you see an expired link message:

1. Return to the login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password** again.
3. Request a new reset email.
4. Use the newest link you receive.

## Password Rules

Your new password must:

* Be at least 10 characters long
* Include at least one uppercase letter
* Include at least one number
* Include at least one special character

Passwords cannot:

* Match any of your last 5 passwords
* Contain your username
* Contain the first part of your email address

## Having Trouble Submitting the Form?

If the password reset page loads but the form will not submit:

1. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
2. Open a private/incognito window.
3. Try the reset process again.

Old browser data is one of the most common causes of password reset issues.
