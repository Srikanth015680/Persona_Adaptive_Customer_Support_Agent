# Mobile App Troubleshooting

This guide covers some of the most common issues reported by CloudSuite mobile app users.

## App Won't Open or Stays on the Loading Screen

If the app is stuck on the splash screen or does not load properly:

1. Close the app completely and reopen it.
2. Make sure your internet connection is working.
3. Open a website in your browser to confirm connectivity.
4. Check the CloudSuite status page for any ongoing service issues.
5. Update the app to the latest version available.
6. If the issue continues, uninstall and reinstall the app.

Reinstalling the app does not remove your account data because your information is stored securely in the cloud.

## Not Receiving Notifications

If push notifications are not arriving:

1. Verify that notifications are enabled in your phone settings.
2. Open the CloudSuite app and check notification settings.
3. Make sure Do Not Disturb or Focus Mode is not blocking alerts.
4. On Android devices, disable battery optimization for CloudSuite if notifications are delayed.

After making changes, restart the app and test again.

## App Crashes

If the app closes unexpectedly:

1. Update the app to the latest version.
2. Restart your device.
3. Clear the app cache if the option is available.
4. Try the same action again.

If the crash continues, collect the following information before contacting support:

* Device model
* Operating system version
* App version
* Steps that cause the crash

Providing these details helps the support team investigate the issue faster.

## Sync Issues Between Mobile and Web

Changes made on mobile and web should normally appear across devices automatically.

If information is not syncing:

1. Confirm your device is connected to the internet.
2. Refresh the app.
3. Check whether the app is currently in offline mode.
4. Wait a few minutes and try again.

If syncing still does not work, contact support with details about the affected records.
