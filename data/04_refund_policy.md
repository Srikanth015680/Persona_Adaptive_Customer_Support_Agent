
# Refund Policy

This article explains when refunds are available and how to submit a refund request.

## Refund Eligibility

New CloudSuite subscriptions are covered by a 14-day money-back guarantee.

If you request a refund within 14 days of your first payment, the refund will be approved automatically and sent back to your original payment method.

Most refunds are processed within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

## Requests After 14 Days

Refund requests submitted after the 14-day period are reviewed by the Billing team.

Refunds may be approved in situations such as:

* Duplicate charges
* Incorrect billing
* Service issues that significantly affected account usage
* Accidental upgrades reported shortly after purchase

Approval is not guaranteed and depends on the details of the request.

## Situations That Usually Do Not Qualify

Refunds are generally not available for:

* Partial use of a billing period after cancellation
* Add-on services that have already been used
* Enterprise agreements with custom billing terms

Enterprise customers should refer to their contract for refund and cancellation terms.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Open  **Billing** .
2. Select  **Invoice History** .
3. Choose the payment you want reviewed.
4. Click  **Request Refund** .
5. Select a reason and submit the request.

Some requests may be approved automatically, while others may be reviewed by a billing specialist.

## When a Request Is Escalated

Certain refund requests require review by a human support agent.

Examples include:

* Duplicate charge disputes
* Chargeback threats
* Unauthorized payment claims
* Refund requests outside the standard policy
* Cases involving account-specific billing concerns

If escalation is required, the request will be forwarded to the Billing team for further review.
