# Cancelling a Subscription

If you no longer need your CloudSuite subscription, you can cancel it at any time from your account settings.

## How to Cancel

1. Open **Billing**.
2. Select **Plan Details**.
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
4. Choose a reason for cancellation (optional).
5. Confirm the cancellation.

Once completed, you'll receive a confirmation email.

## What Happens After Cancellation?

After cancelling:

* Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.
* No future renewal charges will be processed.
* You will continue to have access to all paid features until the subscription expires.

When the billing period ends, the account moves to an archived state.

## Archived Accounts

Archived accounts provide read-only access to historical data for up to 90 days after the subscription ends.

During this period, you can:

* View existing records
* Export your data
* Reactivate your subscription

After 90 days, account data may be permanently removed according to CloudSuite's data retention policy.

## Reactivating a Subscription

You can reactivate your subscription at any time during the active billing period or while the account is archived.

Once payment is completed:

* Full access is restored
* Existing data remains available
* No additional setup is required

## Enterprise Customers

Enterprise subscriptions cannot be cancelled through self-service settings.

If your organization has an Enterprise contract:

* Contact your account manager
* Review any notice requirements in your agreement
* Follow the cancellation process outlined in your contract

## Common Questions

### I Cancelled but Was Still Charged

This usually happens when a renewal charge was processed before the cancellation request was submitted.

Please contact Billing Support for assistance.

### Can I Keep My Data After Cancelling?

Yes. You can export your data before the archive period ends.

See the **Data Export Guide** for instructions.

### I Cancelled by Mistake

You can reactivate your subscription at any time before your data is removed.
