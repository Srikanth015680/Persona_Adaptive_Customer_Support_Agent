
# Clearing Browser Cache and Cookies

Many website issues can be fixed by clearing your browser's cache and cookies.

This helps when pages do not load correctly, forms fail to submit, or you experience repeated login problems.

## Google Chrome

1. Open the Chrome menu.
2. Select **More Tools** →  **Clear Browsing Data** .
3. Choose:
   * Cookies and other site data
   * Cached images and files
4. Click  **Clear Data** .
5. Reload CloudSuite and try again.

## Mozilla Firefox

1. Open  **Settings** .
2. Select  **Privacy & Security** .
3. Under  **Cookies and Site Data** , click  **Clear Data** .
4. Select both available options.
5. Confirm and restart the browser.

## Safari

1. Open  **Safari Settings** .
2. Go to  **Privacy** .
3. Select  **Manage Website Data** .
4. Search for CloudSuite and remove the stored data.
5. Reload the website and sign in again.

## Microsoft Edge

1. Open  **Settings** .
2. Select  **Privacy, Search, and Services** .
3. Under  **Clear Browsing Data** , click  **Choose What to Clear** .
4. Select:
   * Cookies and site data
   * Cached images and files
5. Click  **Clear Now** .

## Try a Private Browser Window

Before clearing everything, you can test CloudSuite in a private browsing session:

* Chrome: Incognito Window
* Firefox: Private Window
* Safari: Private Browsing
* Edge: InPrivate Window

If CloudSuite works correctly in a private window, the issue may be related to cached browser data.

## Still Having Problems?

If the issue continues after clearing cache and cookies:

1. Take a screenshot of the error.
2. Note the steps that caused the issue.
3. Contact support with those details.

Providing screenshots and error messages helps us investigate the problem more quickly.
